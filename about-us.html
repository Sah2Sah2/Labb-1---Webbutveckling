About Us

We’re a team of passionate cat lovers who understand how important your furry family members are. Whether you’re off on an adventure or just need a break from the daily grind, we’re here to give your cats the love, care, and attention they deserve.

We know that cats can be a bit particular (aren’t they all?), so we make sure to follow their routines, play their favorite games, and offer lots of belly rubs when they allow it! 🐾

We’re not just cat-sitters—we're dedicated cat lovers who treat your cats like part of the family. 😻

What makes us even more special? We're a locally-based service in Majorna, so we know the area, and we’re always nearby if you need us. Being part of your neighborhood gives us that extra touch of trustworthiness. You can count on us to be there when you need someone reliable and familiar to take care of your cats.

It’s not just a service—it’s a friendly, local connection in the heart of Majorna!
